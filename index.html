About Yana Mitlitska

Yana Mitlitska was born in Kyiv, Ukraine. After she had graduated from an Art School, she founded an art studio. Being just a hobby at the beginning, it consumed Yana totally very fast. Nowadays Yana is skilled artist with about 10 years of experience in painting. Currently she is working in an art studio in Kyiv.

During her career she collaborated with famous interior designers and painted about 50 oil canvases for appartments in famous Turkey Hotel "Orka Sunrise". In addition her art works presented on exhibitions in Ukraine, Ireland and Turkey. Being fully dedicated to the craft of painting, she creates unique art works and can follow your way of

thinking to create a special composition. She can bring your imagination to a canvas. Yana is known for her shining nature and her ability to adapt to the modern trends and rapidly changing world. The colorfulness of the art works is characterized by mix of colors creating depth and a wide range of the color scale, light and shadow, composition and flow. It's achieved by the years of combining different styles in her personal, unique vision. More ...
